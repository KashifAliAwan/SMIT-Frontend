Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients (Unordered List)

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions (Ordered List)

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  2. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions
  3. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta until crispy
  4. Whisk eggs and grated cheeses together
  5. Drain pasta, reserving some cooking water
  6. Quickly mix hot pasta with egg mixture
  7. Add pancetta and mix well
  8. Season with black pepper and serve immediately

Cooking Terms (Definition List)

Al dente
Pasta cooked to be firm to the bite
Pancetta
Italian cured pork belly
Grate
To reduce food to small shreds by rubbing on a grater

Variations (Nested List)

  • Classic Carbonara
    • Use guanciale instead of pancetta
    • Add extra black pepper
  • Vegetarian Option
    • Substitute pancetta with mushrooms
    • Add garlic for extra flavor
  • Creamy Version
    • Add a splash of cream
    • Use more egg yolks than whites

Additional Notes

  • Always use freshly grated cheese for best results
  • The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs - work quickly!
  • Traditional carbonara doesn't include cream
